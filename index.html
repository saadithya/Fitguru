About AI Fitness Guru

AI Fitness Guru is an innovative mobile application that leverages advanced artificial intelligence to analyze your workout form in real-time. Simply record a video of your exercise, and our AI will provide detailed feedback on your posture, technique, and areas for improvement.

Whether you're a beginner learning proper form or an experienced athlete looking to perfect your technique, AI Fitness Guru provides personalized, professional-grade analysis right from your smartphone.

  1. Configure API Key - Add your Gemini or OpenAI API key in Settings
