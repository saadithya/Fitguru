2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • API Keys: You may provide API keys for Google Gemini or OpenAI services. These keys are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Exercise Names: When submitting a video for analysis, you provide the name of the exercise being performed.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Video Recordings: The App records videos of your workouts using your device's camera. These videos are stored locally on your device.
  • Video Frames: For AI analysis, the App extracts image frames from your recorded videos. These frames are temporarily sent to third-party AI services (Google Gemini or OpenAI) for analysis.
  • Analysis History: Your workout analysis results are stored locally on your device for your review.

2.3 Information NOT Collected

  • We do NOT collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do NOT collect location data
  • We do NOT collect device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • We do NOT use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do NOT store your videos or images on our servers

3. Permissions Required

AI Fitness Guru requires the following device permissions to function:

Permission Purpose Data Usage
CAMERA
(android.permission.CAMERA)		 To record workout videos for AI form analysis Videos are stored locally and frames are sent to AI services only during analysis
RECORD_AUDIO
(android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO)		 Required by Android for video recording functionality Audio is NOT analyzed, stored, or transmitted. This is a technical requirement for video capture.
Important: You can deny these permissions, but the core functionality of recording and analyzing workout videos will not be available.

4. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Workout Analysis: Video frames are sent to AI services (Gemini or OpenAI) to analyze your exercise form and provide feedback.
  • History Tracking: Analysis results are stored locally so you can review your progress over time.
  • App Functionality: To provide the core features of the application.
We do NOT:
  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share your data with third parties except for AI analysis
  • Store your data on our servers

5. Third-Party Services

AI Fitness Guru integrates with the following third-party AI services for workout analysis:

5.1 Google Gemini

When you use Gemini for analysis, video frames are sent to Google's servers. Please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 OpenAI

When you use OpenAI for analysis, video frames are sent to OpenAI's servers. Please review OpenAI's Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy

Note: You provide your own API keys for these services. The data processing by these third-party services is governed by their respective privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies before using these services.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Local Storage

All data including videos, analysis results, and API keys are stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by the operating system.

6.2 Data Transmission

When video frames are sent to AI services for analysis:

  • Data is transmitted over secure HTTPS connections
  • Frames are sent directly to AI service APIs
  • No data passes through our servers

6.3 Data Retention

  • On Your Device: Data remains on your device until you delete the app or manually clear app data
  • AI Services: Please refer to Google and OpenAI privacy policies for their data retention practices
  • Our Servers: We do not retain any of your data

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: All your data is stored locally on your device and is accessible through the app
  • Deletion: You can delete your workout history within the app, or uninstall the app to remove all data
  • Permission Control: You can revoke camera and microphone permissions at any time through your device settings
  • API Key Management: You can remove or change your API keys at any time in the app settings
